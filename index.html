About Me

My name is Jonathan Mathai and I'm currently a computer science major on a premedical track at Stony Brook University. I'm a Long Island native and have thoroughly enjoyed my time growing up here on the island. Before coming into college I wasn't entirely sure about my career path and where exactly I wanted to go with my life in the future. Choosing to major in computer science was a choice I made in part to seeing some of my friends in high school code. Seeing the software they were able to produce as the fruits of their labor piqued my interest in the field of computer science.

At Stony Brook, I want to maximize most of my time into becoming the ideal person I wish to see myself as in the future. I wish to explore the many opportunities that the university has to offer and I hope to develop lifelong relationships here that I will carry with me after college.

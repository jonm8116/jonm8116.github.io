Early Years

I was born here in New York at the Long Island Jewish hospital on August 31st 1997. My family initially lived in the small community of Glen Oaks and attended kindergarten at Notre Dame. After Notre Dame my parents sent me to the Garden City Park elementary school from first grade to sixth. While I was at the school I was part of the Odyssey and Math enrichment program. During these years I got more involved in playing soccer and played in leagues like wildcats and for church leagues.

High School

After sixth grade I made the transition to high school and from this point onward I had to think about the next step in my future. This is when the idea of college first entered my mind. So I began to challenge myself a little more academically and physically within school. However even though I may have challenged myself I was unable to accomplish some goals and fell short in some areas but I hope to use this as motivation for my future.

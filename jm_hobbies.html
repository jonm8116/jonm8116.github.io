Courage

Darkness grows, it enters through the door
It surrounds me, seeping its way through the cracks on the floor
The abscence of light, it scares me, it terrifies
But it can do more because within me it creates a divide

It influences me, it restrains me
It takes my brain and all it does is drain me
Of my hope and leaves me paralyzed
I am stuck in the void, I'm petrified

This darkness, it's too much for me to bear
I cannot do anything so long as it stares
Hope enters

I can move, I can see
A signal of light that is the source of my glee
Shows me a way out, a route of escape
But where will it bring me, where will it take?
Should I follow, should I stay?

The light beckons
I give chase as the darkness pursues
It will attack me within seconds
But I turn around, I see it had withdrew
The light, my courage had pulled me through

Finally free
I am no longer held by balls and chains
I can do anything that I please

So I must not let fear take a hold of me
Because it can change who I am to be
Rather let courage to help me
Become the man I want to be

The Escape

Walking swiftly through the woods, Eyeing the Beast which haunted his nightmares,
Trying to bring him, within its grasp,
He fled.

Moving quickly to escape its clutches. Until he found a bridge
His symbol of hope
He walked across
One foot in front of the other
He approached the other side
His source of salvation

Only to face his demise
For all he could see
Was the Beast
Staring at him through its cold desolate eyes
